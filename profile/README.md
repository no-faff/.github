# No Faff

Small, useful software. No accounts, no subscriptions, no bloat.

### InstallerClean

Windows has a hidden folder called `C:\Windows\Installer` that grows forever and never gets cleaned up. InstallerClean finds the files that are no longer needed and lets you safely reclaim the space. Most people get back a few GB. Some get back tens of GB. Open source replacement for PatchCleaner (last updated 2016).

### Simple Browser Picker

Registers as your default browser and shows a picker every time a link is opened. You choose which browser (and which profile) it opens in. Supports every Chromium and Firefox-family browser, including profiles. Rules let you route specific sites silently without being asked. Single exe, nothing to install.

### Smart Auto Refresh & Notify

Auto-refreshes a browser tab on a timer. Optionally plays a sound when specific text appears or disappears on the page. Handy for monitoring pages, waiting for tickets to drop or keeping dashboards fresh. Chrome and Firefox.

### Simple Video Downloader

Download video and audio from the browser. Pick your format, queue up multiple downloads, let it get on with it. Powered by yt-dlp. Firefox.

### Transcribe

Transcribe audio and video locally on your own machine using Whisper. Nothing gets uploaded anywhere. GPU-accelerated, supports speaker detection and multiple output formats.
